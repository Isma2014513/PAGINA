Name
Lloyd Frontera
Description of character
Lloyd Frontera is a leading construction engineer responsible for the development of the Frontera County. He is the eldest son of Arcos and Marbella Frontera, the older brother of Julian Frontera, and the main protagonist of The Greatest Estate Developer. His body is currently inhabited by Suho Kim. The fate of his original soul is unknown.
Name
Alicia Termina Magentano
Description of character
Alicia Termina Magentano is the reigning empress currently residing in the royal capital of Magenta. She holds the highest authority in the land as well as absolute power with her overwhelming charisma, intelligence, and strength. In the events of Knight of Blood and Iron, Alicia is said to become a ruthless, bloodthirsty tyrant following the betrayal of one dear to her.
Name
Javier Asrahan
Description of character
Javier Asrahan is a loyal knight of the Frontera family. He is the protagonist of Knight of Blood and Iron and the deuteragonist of The Greatest Estate Developer.
He currently acts as the personal guard of Lloyd Frontera and accompanies him on nearly every occasion.
Name
Serajard
Description of character
Serajard is the fourth daughter of Samarkand and a princess of Asfahan. Alongside her sisters, her identity as a princess is hidden from everyone outside of the palace. In the events of Knight of Blood and Iron, Serajard was killed while protecting her father from rebels.
(is in love with Julian Frontera)
Name
Julian Frontera
Description of character
Julian Frontera is a student at the Magentano Royal Academy. He is the son of Arcos and Marbella Frontera as well as the younger brother of Lloyd Frontera. In the events of Knight of Blood and Iron, Julian was evicted from the academy dorms due to his family's inability to pay tuition and was killed by a thief while wandering the streets.
(is in love with Serajard)
